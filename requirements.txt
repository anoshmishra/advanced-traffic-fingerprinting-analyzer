# 🚦 Advanced Traffic Fingerprinting Analyzer

[![Python](https://img.shields.io/badge/Python-3.8%2B-blue.svg)](https://www.python.org/)
[![License: MIT](https://img.shields.io/badge/License-MIT-yellow.svg)](LICENSE)
[![Build Status](https://img.shields.io/github/actions/workflow/status/anoshmishra/advanced-traffic-fingerprinting-analyzer/ci.yml?branch=main)](https://github.com/anoshmishra/advanced-traffic-fingerprinting-analyzer/actions)

An advanced toolkit for **encrypted traffic fingerprinting analysis**, combining **GUI visualization**, **machine learning models**, and **network security tools** to study, detect, and simulate encrypted flow behaviors.

---

## ✨ Features

- 🔍 Encrypted flow **metadata extraction**
- 📊 **Visualization dashboard** (interactive GUI)
- 🤖 **ML/DL integration** (scikit-learn, XGBoost, TensorFlow, PyTorch)
- 🌐 **Network condition simulation** & security modules (Scapy, Nmap, Paramiko)
- 📑 **Report generation** in PDF/HTML
- 🛠️ Configurable & extensible pipeline

---
